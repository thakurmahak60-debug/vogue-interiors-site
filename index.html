Sheet A—06 / Method

How a project runs.

Five stages, in order — because the sequence itself avoids rework later.

STEP 01

Consultation

An on-site or in-studio conversation about the space, the budget and how you intend to use each room.

STEP 02

Space Planning

Measured drawings, layout options and circulation studies — the plan is agreed before design starts.

STEP 03

Concept & Materials

Mood, palette, furniture and material selections presented against the approved plan.

STEP 04

Execution

Civil work, carpentry, electrical and finishing managed on site under one project timeline.

STEP 05

Handover

Final styling, snag checks and walkthrough before the space is handed back to you.
